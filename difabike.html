Difabike - Transportation Mobile App

Overview

DifaBike is a transportation provider likes motorcycle taxis initiated and driven by people with disabilities and specifically designed for them as well. This startup was initiated to facilitate transportation for people with disabilities, and also to provide job opportunities for them who usually get discrimination. In this project, the DifaBike Team needed a mobile app to accommodate these activities.
